# Preface

Here a few blog posts and opinions of developers in the space actively thinking about how to apply these tools to our work. It's still early days, so there's not much formality yet. But, I think the gut feelings and initial impressions of experienced developers is valuable. Perhaps even moreso than benchmarks and success rates at this point in time. It's worth examining opinions from both those in support and against using these tools.
